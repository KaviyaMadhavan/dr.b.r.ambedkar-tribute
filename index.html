Dr.B.R.Ambedkar

  • B.R.Ambedkar was born on 14th April 1891 in Mhow,Madhya Pradesh.
  • His full name was Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar.
  • He cleared matriculation from the Elphinstone High School in 1908
  • Studied Economics from the Columbia University, New York
  • Awarded Doctorate degree in Economics by the University of Columbia on 8th June 1927
  • He was one of the crusaders of caste discrimination and untouchability.
  • Launched a newspaper called Mooknayaka in 1920
  • He was appointed as the first Law Minister of independent India
  • He was made the chairman of the constitutional drafting commitee and considered as the cheif architect of Indian constitution.
  • He was awarded the prestigious Bharat Ratna award in 1990.
  • He died on 6th December 1956 at his home in Delhi
